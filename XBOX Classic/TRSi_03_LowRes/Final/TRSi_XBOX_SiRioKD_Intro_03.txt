Page Texter
-----------

Addresses: 0x35908=Page1, 0x35AA0=Page2,  0x35C38=Page3,

Every page is 40x8 uppercase chars.
matrix = " ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ1234567890()+,!'@/><:.-"
 
* Special chars:
'q' long wait
'w' short wait


ScrollText

* Special chars:
'w' restart
